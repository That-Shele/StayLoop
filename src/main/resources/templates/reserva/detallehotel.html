About this stay

Hotel Grand Vista, nestled in the heart of San Francisco, offers a blend of historic charm and modern luxury. Originally built in 1920, the hotel has been meticulously restored to its former glory, featuring elegant architecture and sophisticated interiors. Guests can enjoy spacious rooms with stunning city views, a rooftop pool, and a gourmet restaurant serving locally sourced cuisine. The hotel is conveniently located near Union Square, offering easy access to shopping, dining, and cultural attractions. Whether you're here for business or leisure, Hotel Grand Vista provides an unforgettable experience.
